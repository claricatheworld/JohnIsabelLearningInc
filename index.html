PROVIDES QUALITY EDUCATION TO BRING OUT THE BEST IN EVERY CHILD.

MISSION

John Isabel Learning Center’s main goal is to
give quality education which is not limited
to the child’s academic development alone
but also the continuous development of his/ her
physical, moral, social and spiritual being, so that
he/ she will grow as a well rounded individual,
capable of helping the society to achieve its
aspirations intelligently.

VISION

The objectives of JILC is to make each pupil special
by allowing him/ her to express himself/ herself
freely; and to make each child feel that his/ her
teacher is his/ her special friend and counselor.

JILC’s vision is to yield pupils with the vital
characteristics needed to put education
to use for the good of the society, armed with
spirit that compels them in God’s world.
